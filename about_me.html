About Me

Hi, My name is Jenne Stamplecoskie. I am a recent graduate of Computer Programming at Algonquin College. Prior to that I spent 21 years in the Canadian Armed Forces as an Aerospace Engineer. In my military life, I was an Aerospace Engineer by trade. An Electrical Engineer by degree, specializing in Digital Signal Processing. I spent much of my career working in Electronic Warfare, where among other things, I had a lot of data analysis work to do. As we did not have the tools to perform the analysis, I started to build some. This allowed me to analyse faster, process greater quantities of data, and find patterns I would not have been able to visualize otherwise. Thus began my interest in computer programming.
